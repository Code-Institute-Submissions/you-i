Hello!
I’m Danielle, I’m a personal trainer and You&i Fitness is my business!
You&i Fitness all started 8 years ago when I made the decision to make my passion and my hobby into my dream job! I came up with the name You&i Fitness because it’s a journey that myself and the client are on together. I hold my sessions in a private 1-1 studio & it is just “you and I” Fitness is something I am extremely passionate about, I go to the gym at least 5-6 times per week and having a healthy lifestyle is something I promote everyday!
I became a personal trainer because I could see the struggle some people have to get the motivation to go. I couldn’t think of anything more rewarding than helping them reach there goals, but not only that, helping them become the best version of them self that they can be!
I hope you enjoy the website and remember - Stop Wishing. Start Doing!
